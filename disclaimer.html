EjeConnect Foundation (referred as ‘EjeConnect’) is an organization which functions with the aim to promote voluntary blood donation and spread awareness among the people to donate blood which is helpful for the poor and needy.

Contacting Blood Donors The users (‘Requesters’) shall raise a request on the website. The EjeConnect team shall match the requester with the nearest registered donors. The team would try to arrange for donors for the required blood group. However, EjeConnect does not take any sort of guarantee of assurance of providing donors.

EjeConnect volunteers are experts in the field of arranging donors and shall give in full efforts to handle the request effectively.

EjeConnect shall not be held liable for injury, loss of life, or any other way in case it is unable to arrange for donors.

The details of the donors will be shared with the requester once the EjeConnect team is able to arrange for a donor.

EjeConnect shall not be held liable in case a donor refuses to donate blood at any stage.

EjeConnect DO NOT pay the donors either monetary or otherwise for donating blood. Blood donation is expected to be done for free without any expectation for a reward.
